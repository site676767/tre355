Meridian Aero Leasing (FZE)

A UAE-based aviation company focused on aircraft charter solutions, leasing and rental services, aviation equipment rental, and aviation-related support for private, corporate, and commercial clients.

Company Overview

Registration Details

Company Registration: Sharjah Free Zone, United Arab Emirates

Registered Address: SAIF Office Q1-09-007/A, Sharjah Airport International Free Zone (SAIF Zone), Sharjah, United Arab Emirates

Shareholder / Managing Director: Mr. Stefan Rosina

Overview

Meridian Aero Leasing (FZE) is established to develop business opportunities in the aviation sector by providing professional aviation solutions to clients and partners.

All aviation activities are intended to be carried out in compliance with applicable UAE regulations and subject to relevant approvals, permits, and licenses.

Business Activities

Core Services

  • Aircraft Charter Services
  • Aircraft Leasing and Rental Solutions
  • Aviation Equipment Rental
  • Aviation-related Support Services

Business Model

The company operates through cooperation with aircraft owners, aviation operators, service providers, and industry partners.

  • Facilitating aircraft charter arrangements
  • Providing aircraft rental solutions
  • Supporting clients with aviation asset access
  • Developing partnerships in the international aviation market

Markets and Scope

Private Clients Executive aviation solutions for individuals.
Corporate Clients Flexible aviation support for companies and business groups.
International Reach Services aimed at clients and partners worldwide.
UAE Base Strategic location with strong aviation infrastructure.

Management
